Privacy Statement for Plang

  1. Introduction:

    At Plang, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Statement outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your personal information.

  2. Data Collection:

    Users can choose to participate in our data collection. If opted in, we collect data sent to us to improve our algorithm. This data is stored for a maximum of 7 days. No personal data is required for registration. However, any personal data submitted while using the service is stored but deleted after 7 days. Purchasing a voucher requires certain personal information such as Name and credit card details. While Plang does not store this information, our payment gateway, Rapyd, might retain it. If a user opts out of data collection, we do not store any of their data. Data only remains on our servers for the duration required to process it.

  3. Data Usage:

    The collected data is used solely to improve our algorithm for code completion.

  4. Data Sharing:

    For AI processing, we share data with OpenAI.

  5. Data Protection:

    We employ the latest technology to ensure the security of user data. This includes firewalls and running servers in containers for isolation.

  6. User Rights:

    Users have the right to request the deletion of their data at any time.

  7. Cookies:

    We do not use cookies or any other tracking technologies.

  8. Third-Party Links:

    Our service integrates with OpenAI for data processing. Users are advised to review OpenAI's privacy policy, as our privacy statement does not extend to third-party sites.

  9. Children's Privacy:

    We do not intentionally collect data from individuals under a certain age.

  10. Contact Information:

    For questions or concerns about your privacy, please refer to the contact details provided on our website.

  11. Changes to the Privacy Statement:

    Any changes to this Privacy Statement will be communicated to users through our built-in messaging system.
